I'm a Computer Science student at Santa Monica College, working towards an AA degree with an emphasis in web programming. I originally majored in Philosophy and earned a BA from UCLA in 2012.

I first became interested in computers as an 8 year-old trying to figure out Windows 95. My motivators were frustration and an incessant need to solve puzzles. I was blown away when I figured out shortcuts like ctrl+c and v. Ctrl+alt+del was a godsend in the early days. Even though this only scratches the surface of computing concepts, it made me realize just how deep the whole thing was, so I've been digging ever since.
